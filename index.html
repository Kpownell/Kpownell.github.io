Get Started!

Ever wanted to learn electrical engineering but didn't know where to begin? Well, you've come to the right place! Arduino World is a site dedicated to educating people about this microcontroller from the ground up. Never heard of Ohm's Law? Start with the basics - electricity, microcontrollers, and the basic hardware we'll be using. Processing pro? Check out some of the advanced projects or expand your knowledge with additional add-on shields. All ages are welcome to explore and enjoy. Ready? Let's get started!
