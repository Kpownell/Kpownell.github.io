About


This website was created as a resource to help beginners learn Arduino from the ground up. The content will hopefully contribute to educating a new generation of budding electrical engineers and hobbyists who just want to learn a bit more about the maker movement.


Kristen Pownell is an electrical engineering student at Stanford University who learned about Arduino during a freshman introductory seminar. She got the idea for this website while mentoring a fifth grade student in conjunction with Stanford Society of Women Engineers. She hopes her work will have a positive impact for future mentors and mentees.


Comments? Questions? Email me for more information. Feedback is always welcome!
